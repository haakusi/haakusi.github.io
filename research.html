00 / PRINCIPLE

A novel method is useful only when the comparison is fair.

The recurring thread between my engineering and research is verification. I keep the problem definition, classical baselines, data split, budget, metrics, and failure cases visible before interpreting a result.

실무와 연구를 잇는 공통 원칙은 검증입니다. 결과를 해석하기 전에 문제 정의, 고전 기준선, 데이터 분할, 예산, 지표와 실패 사례를 명시합니다.

01 / ACTIVE RESEARCH

Two questions, one evaluation discipline

TRACK 01

Explainable Traditional-Dance Retrieval & Recommendation

RESEARCH IN PROGRESS

Question

Can expert domain knowledge and visual-language models retrieve and recommend traditional-dance material while explaining which movement, costume, formation, rhythm, or cultural cues support the result?

Proposed approach

Represent expert annotations and multimodal evidence separately, then combine them at retrieval and explanation time so the contribution of domain knowledge can be audited.

Evaluation focus

Retrieval relevance, expert agreement, explanation faithfulness, and failure analysis across ambiguous or culturally similar material.

TRACK 02

Hybrid Quantum–Classical Model Selection

FRAMEWORK STUDY

Question

Under which data, feature, noise, and resource conditions should a model-selection workflow prefer a classical baseline, a quantum kernel, or a variational quantum model?

Comparison contract

Compare classical baselines and QML candidates with the same data splits, budgets, and evaluation gates. Record accuracy, robustness, runtime, resource cost, and instability rather than assuming quantum advantage.

Intended contribution

A reproducible selection framework that can recommend a model family—or reject all candidates—based on evidence from the task and execution environment.

03 / WORKING METHOD

How I keep a research claim honest

  1. 01
    Define the decision

    State what the result must help a person or system decide.

  2. 02
    Freeze the comparison

    Keep splits, budgets, metrics, and baselines shared before experimentation.

  3. 03
    Record failures

    Treat instability, ambiguity, and negative results as evidence, not cleanup work.

  4. 04
    Separate status from ambition

    Label work as proposed, in progress, evaluated, submitted, or published.
